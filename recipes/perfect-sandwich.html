The Perfect Sandwich

A Large and in charge sandwich blessed by the gods.It is said whomever makes this sandwich is making the will of space and time. To eat this sandwich is to know the true meaning of existence, aswell as the true meaning of despair. Be warned, to consume the sandwich is to erase lifes pleasure for the knowledge of greatest and return to its dissapointments...

Ingredients:

  • Baguette
  • Cream cheese
  • Slice Pickles (source: Prismo)
  • Dill
  • Diced boiled eggs
  • Bird (source: From the window)
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Sliced Roma tomatoes
  • Sliced Sweet yellow onion
  • Tears (for salt)
  • Boneless ribeye
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Bacon
  • A lobster's soul
  1. Prepare sous-vide water to 135 degrees. While water is warming up wash your vegtables.
  2. When the water is finished place the a vaccum sealed bag with the boneless ribeye,rosemary, and time inside for 2 hours.
  3. Obtain a bird from any nearby window and cook to your perfered method.
  4. Locate and befriend a lobster.Cherish it,Love it, and once you have perform the soul stealing ritual of the gods of old.
  5. After obtaining the lobster soul quickly collect you tears for use later. If you dont have any consult with you nearest therapist
  6. eWhen the ribeye is finished pull it out and let it rest
  7. While ribeye is resting bake your bacon at 350 degrees until crispy
  8. Cut and toast your baguette. Then spread cream cheese evenly on bread.
  9. Layer your ingredients in the following order:
    1. Pickles
    2. Dill
    3. Diced boiled eggs
    4. Bird from the window
    5. Sliced cucumbers
    6. Sliced Roma tomatoes
    7. Sliced Sweet yellow onion
    8. Tears
    9. Ribeye
    10. Bacon
    11. Lobster soul
  10. Behold your glorious creation and enjoy!
