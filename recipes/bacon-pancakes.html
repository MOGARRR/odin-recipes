this is bacon pancakes

Pancakes are a beautiful sight to behold in the morning, yet there is one thing is even better to witness...Bacon pancakes! Embued with the savory and fatty goodness of bacon, these pancakes are elevated to prestige few will ever know. Much like the sword in the stone, these pancakes will test the will and character of any who choose to face the delicous golden mound. Do you have what it takes?

ingredients

  • 1 Cup AP flour
  • 2 Tbs sugar
  • 1 1/2 tps baking powder
  • 1/2 tps baking soda
  • 1/2 coarse salt
  • 1 1/4 cups butter milk
  • 2 Tbs melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 10 slices of bacon (source: The Gods)
  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees. Line bacon on a tray with patchment paper and bake for 5 mins on each side. You may be apporached by an eldritch being with further advice, take it for they make amazing bacon.
  2. While the bacon is cooking, mix together all of your dry ingredients into a bowl.
  3. In a seperate bowl place your wet ingredients together. Think of all the people who will never experince these pancakes, and with the fury of this injustice whisk the wet ingredients.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients together.
  5. Over a pan on medium heat place down bacon with room for the pancake batter. Place about 1/4 cup of pancake batter over each strip of bacon.
  6. Cook for about two minutes and flip onto the other side with a spatula. Cook for another two mintues or until the bottom is golden brown.
  7. Place you pancakes ontop of each other and if the gods find it satisfactory they will bless your pancakes with maple syrup.
  8. Enjoy the fruit of your spirtual journey.
